# Lab 1: Introduction

## Research the following questions:

\[ [Lab Table of Contents](./README.md#labs) \]

As a lab team or individually research the following questions (all sources):

* Define a block structure of systems in an airframe?
* What differentiates MIL-STD-1553 different from Ethernet, Token Ring, FireWire, USB 3, Thunderbolt, etc.?
* What advantages does 1Mbps MIL-STD-1553 have over other network technologies?
* Why and how are other networks used in the airframe, and which ones?
* What obstacles and who stood in the way of MIL-STD-1553?
* What are "notices" to MIL-STD-1553 and where does the name "notice" come from?
* How are failures isolated in military airframes?
* What movement is there to fiber-optics in general in the airframe, and with MIL-STD-1553?
* What other standards are related to MIL-STD-1553?
